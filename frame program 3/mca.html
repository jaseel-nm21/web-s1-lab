department of computer application

A Department of Computer Application (DCA) provides education in computer science principles and practical skills to prepare students for careers in the IT and software industries, with programs like Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) and Master of Computer Applications (MCA). These departments aim to equip students with theoretical knowledge, software development skills, and professional ethics, often including well-equipped labs and emphasizing real-world project work. Their goals include fostering technical competence, promoting research, and developing globally competitive professionals for the evolving digital world
